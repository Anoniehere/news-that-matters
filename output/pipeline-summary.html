The 3 core intelligence bets this product makes
🧠
Meaning over keywords

"Federal Reserve" and "the Fed" are the same thing. "Hike rates" and "raise benchmark" are the same action. A keyword matcher misses this. Our neural model doesn't. That's 3× more events detected on the same data.

📰
Editorial consensus as signal

If the Economy desk, the Politics desk, and Top Stories all independently surface the same story — three editorial teams agreed. That's a stronger trending signal than any algorithm alone.

🎯
Persona-calibrated insight

"Why it matters" isn't generic. It's written for a 32-year-old Silicon Valley PM or early investor who needs to sound sharp in tomorrow's leadership sync — not a general reader.
